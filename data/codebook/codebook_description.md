# Topic Codebook — ICE Behavioral Literature Pipeline

This codebook was used for LLM-based classification of expert
annotations in the validation step of the pipeline.
Changing any definition will change the kappa result.

## Topic 0: Space Mission & Crew Dynamics
BERTopic label: 0_space_mission_crew_group
Top keywords: space, mission, crew, group, study, social, antarctic
Documents: 37 of 63 validated documents
Description: Papers studying psychological and social behavior
of crews in space missions and Antarctic analog environments.
